## 4. Bank Reconciliation system (NEW)
New **🔄 Reconcile** button on the Banking page. Upload a statement (**CSV / Excel / PDF**) → it matches against the recorded ledger and shows what's unrecorded, outstanding, and any unexplained gap; one-click **Add** posts a missing entry.

- **New file:** `nasama-accounting-v2.reconcile.jsx` (loaded in `index.html` between invoices.jsx and pages.jsx).
- `index.html` — added pdf.js CDN (`pdf.js/3.11.174`) + the reconcile script tag.
- `pages.jsx` — `showReconcile` state, header button (gated `accounting.read`), modal render in `BankingPageV2`.
- **Parsers:** CSV/TSV (`parseDelimitedRows`), Excel (global `XLSX`), PDF (pdf.js, best-effort). Auto-detects the header row + columns (Date/Description/Debit/Credit/Amount/Balance), all overridable under **Columns ▾**. Auto-fills the statement closing balance.
- **Matching:** two-pass — exact amount + date window, then **near-amount** within a tolerance. Buckets: Matched / Near matches / Unrecorded (on statement, not in books) / Outstanding (in books, not on statement, within the statement's date window).
- **Math:** `Book + unrecorded − outstanding + near-diffs = expected`, compared to the actual statement balance; the leftover is flagged **Unexplained** (✓ Reconciled when 0).
- **Tolerances:** Date default **±7 days** (bank value dates lag; options up to ±30). Amount default **±1.00 AED** (pairs rounding/fee/typo differences and shows the exact gap).

### Reconciliation refinements (13 Jun — all in reconcile.jsx)
- **Control totals panel (the headline):** total Money In / Money Out / Net — bank statement vs books over the period, with a ✓/✗ verdict. This is the **reliable aggregate check** (immune to splits & duplicates) and the user's stated priority ("total debit & credit should agree"). Anchor on this, not the line lists.
- **Near-match (default ±1.00 AED amount tolerance):** pairs items whose amounts differ slightly and shows the gap. Real case: program broker payment **18,666.55** vs bank **18,666.00** → near match **+0.55**. → DECIDE: typo vs real variance.
- **Date tolerance default widened 4 → 7 days** (bank value dates lag; options up to ±30).
- **⚠ possible-duplicate flag** on Outstanding items whose amount already matched elsewhere.
- **Group / split matching (3rd pass, bounded subset-sum):** one entry ↔ several lines that sum to it. Verified real cases: book **19,600** Skycourts receipt ↔ bank **10,000 + 9,600** cheques; one **−210** book fee ↔ bank **−200 + −10** (fee + VAT).
- **Duplicate finder (new "⚠ Duplicates" button on Banking):** scans bank/cash txns for same-amount (±tolerance) + near-date copies; flags **imported + manual** mixes (the classic double-entry) and lets you **Void** the extra. Defaults ±1 AED / ±2 days / "only mixes" → surfaces real dups (e.g. imported −289.79 + manual −289.00 typo) and hides recurring-charge noise; widen the window to ±5–7 to also catch date-shifted dups like the 19,600. This is the tool to knock down the 4,596.80.

### Data-hygiene finding (the real source of the noise)
The user is **both importing the bank statement AND manually entering some receipts/payments** → the same money is recorded twice (e.g. imported `SR-MPR4KHET` 26 May + manual `SR-MPSFF12L` 30 May; plus the 30 May split). This double-entry is the main cause of the reconciliation noise and part of the **4,596.80** gap. Fixes: stop double-entering, delete duplicates, and trust the Control totals.
